Command Description

/ah Open the Auction House GUI
/ah sell <price> List the item in your hand for sale

Land Claiming Guide

  1. Hold a Golden Shovel.
  2. Right-click two opposite corners of a rectangle (ensure they are at the same block level).
  3. Use a Stick to right-click and verify your boundaries (shown in gold).

Limit: Up to 10,000 blocks for free (Min 81 blocks). Use /abandonclaim to start over.

Permissions

  • /untrust <name> - Revoke access
