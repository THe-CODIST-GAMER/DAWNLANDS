Command Description

/jobs leaveall Reset your Jobs
/pay <player> Transfer money to another player
/ah Open the Auction House GUI
/ah sell <price> List the item in your hand for sale

Quick Join Job

Note: You are allowed to take up multiple jobs simultaneously.

Land Claiming (upto 10000 blocks of area) Guide

  1. Hold a Golden Shovel.
  2. Right-click two opposite corners of your desired rectangular area , ensure they are of the same level.
  3. Use a Stick to right-click and verify your boundaries , and you can also see others boundaries by this if you aim the stick on their land.

Management Commands

  • /trust <name> - Give a player access to your land
  • /trustlist - View everyone trusted on your claim
  • /untrust <name> - Revoke access for a player
  • /claimlist - View all your claimed lands
  • /abandonclaim - Delete current claim
